Privacy Policy for VacationCostTracker

Last updated: March 6, 2026

**Overview**

VacationCostTracker is a personal expense tracking application for iOS. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

**Data We Access**

VacationCostTracker connects to your bank accounts and credit cards through Plaid to retrieve transaction data, including merchant names, transaction amounts, dates, and location information where available.

**How Your Data Is Used**

Transaction data is used solely to populate your personal expense tracker. The app matches transactions to your travel dates and destinations to help you track spending during trips.

**Data Storage**

Trip data, destinations, and expenses are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
Bank connection credentials are never stored in this app. Plaid handles authentication securely on your behalf.
A minimal backend server stores only the Plaid item identifier (not your bank credentials) necessary to retrieve transactions on your behalf.
Data Sharing

Your financial data is not sold, shared, or disclosed to any third parties. Data is only transmitted between your device and your personal backend server for the purpose of syncing transactions.

**Third-Party Services**

This app uses Plaid (plaid.com) to connect to financial institutions. Plaid's privacy policy governs how Plaid handles your data during the connection process.

**Data Deletion**

You can disconnect any bank at any time from within the app (Settings → Bank Connection → Remove). This deletes the connection from both the app and the backend server.

**Contact**

This is a personal application. For questions, contact the developer directly.
